COMPUTATION OF SGPA

The following procedure is to be adopted to compute the Semester Grade Point Average (SGPA).
The SGPA is the ratio of sum of the product of the number of credits with the grade points scored by student in all the courses taken by the student and the sum of the number of credits of all the courses undergone by a student.

SGPA(Si) = Σ(Ci Gi) / ΣCi

Where Ci is the credits of the i-th course and Gi is the credits scored by the student for the i-th course.

COMPUTATION OF CGPA

The CGPA is also calculated in the same way, Taking account all the courses undergone by a student in all the semesters.

CGPA(Ci) = Σ(Ci Si) / ΣCi

Where Si is the SGPA of the i-th semester and Ci is the total number of credits in that semester.
