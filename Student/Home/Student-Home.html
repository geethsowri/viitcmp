Student Course Management Portal

Attendance Tracking

Never miss a beat with our intuitive attendance tracking feature. Log in to the student portal to view your attendance records for each class, ensuring you stay on top of your commitments. With this tool, you can effortlessly keep track of your attendance progress and make informed decisions regarding your academic performance.

Grade Monitoring

Stay informed about your academic progress through our comprehensive grade monitoring system. Access your grades online, view detailed grade breakdowns, and gain valuable insights into your performance in individual courses. Our user-friendly interface makes it simple to track your progress, empowering you to make the necessary adjustments to achieve your academic goals.

Study Materials

At VIIT, we believe in providing our students with the tools they need to excel. The student portal grants you exclusive access to a wealth of study materials curated by our esteemed lecturers. From lecture notes to presentations, study guides to additional resources, everything you need to enhance your understanding and preparation is just a click away.

Syllabus Check

Never be caught off guard by course requirements and expectations again. Our student portal provides easy access to the semester syllabus for each of your enrolled courses. Familiarize yourself with the course objectives, assessment criteria, and recommended reading materials, giving you a clear roadmap to success in your academic pursuits.
